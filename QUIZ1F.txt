QUIZ 1F

ASUS PC

OBSERVATION:
	The system unit is not turning on but the LED is. CPU fan and chassis fan are working but after a few seconds, it stopped.
The unit is covered with dust but the RAM looks clean. The capacitor in the power supply unit is bloated and the fuse is blown.

POSSIBLE PROBLEM:
	The power supply unit might be a problem because the unit does not boot completely.

POSSIBLE SOLUTIONS:
	Clean the whole power supply unit and system unit.
	Check all the connections in the motherboard and hard drive.
	If possible, try replacing the blown components in the PSU or try another PSU that is compatible with the system unit.
